Soweto Community Awareness Engagement

In Soweto, Kahawa West, Trash Mtaani's community engagement will be significantly amplified through their collaboration with Kaa Sober, a local Community-Based Organization (CBO). This partnership will mean that Kaa Sober, with its existing community ties and understanding of local dynamics, will play a crucial role in mobilizing residents for waste management initiatives. Their activities will involve facilitating awareness campaigns, organizing community clean-up drives, and establishing or strengthening local waste collection points. Kaa Sober's involvement will also help in fostering a sense of ownership and responsibility among residents, encouraging them to actively participate in waste segregation at source and explore income-generating opportunities through recycling, thereby ensuring the sustainability and success of the waste management efforts in this low-income neighborhood.
